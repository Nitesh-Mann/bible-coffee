Return Policy

Bible & Coffee Planner will accept returns on product within 30 calendar days from the date of purchase if the product:

  • Never used
  • All contents are in their original condition: the pages are not written on, not torn, and the cover is not damaged.

To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it. Your item MUST be unopened and in the original packaging. When sending an return back to our facility be sure to notate the order number on the return label or on a note included in the box.
